About Me

I am a graduate of the Software Guild's intensive 12-week course in Java and Fullstack Development. Given the fast pace of the program, I learned to complete projects in an efficient framework in order to meet hard deadlines. The final project, Blog / Content Management System, required collaborating with a team, sharing ideas and utilizing SCRUM methodology to ensure work flow was in order and efficient. I am eager to continue learning from and contributing to a software development team.

After the Software Guild I landed a 6-month internship at Quicksilver Express Courier where I utilized all the skills that was learned from the Guild. Expanding on Agile work development in a DevOps work environment. For the most part I was tasked with refactoring the old website for customers account and order entry to more updated version.

Now I am currently studying at the Saint Paul College to get my 2 year AAS in computer programming
