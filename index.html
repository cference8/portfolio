About Me

I am a recent graduate of the Software Guild's intensive 12-week course in Java and Fullstack Development. During the program, I developed the ability to complete projects quickly and efficiently in order to meet tight deadlines. The final project involved working collaboratively with a team, exchanging ideas, and utilizing SCRUM methodology to ensure effective work flow. I am eager to continue developing my skills and contributing to a software development team.

Following the Software Guild, I secured a 6-month internship at Quicksilver Express Courier. During this time, I applied the skills and knowledge acquired from the Guild to an Agile development environment with DevOps practices. My primary responsibility was to refactor the existing customer account and order entry website to a more up-to-date version.

I am currently pursuing an Associate of Applied Science in Computer Programming at Saint Paul College and expect to graduate at the end of 2023.
