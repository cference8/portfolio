Our Services

Nail Trim Service

At Sunshine, we understand that maintaining your dog's nails can be a daunting task. That's why we offer professional nail trimming services to keep your furry friend's paws healthy and comfortable. Our expert groomers will carefully clip and file your dog's nails to prevent overgrowth, breakage, and other nail-related issues.

Full Cut and Color

For pet owners who love to add a bit of style and flair to their furry companions, our full cut and color service is the perfect option. Our professional groomers can give your dog a fresh new look with a haircut and coloring that suits their personality. We use only pet-safe products for coloring and styling, so you can be sure your furry friend is in good hands.

Shed-less Treatment

If you're tired of constantly cleaning up your dog's hair around the house, then our shed-less treatment might be just what you need. Our treatment can help reduce shedding by up to 90%. It includes a thorough brushing, shampooing, and conditioning, followed by a blowout and de-shedding treatment.

Deep Conditioning Treatment

Just like humans, dogs' hair can get damaged and dry. Our deep conditioning treatment can help restore your dog's coat to its natural luster and shine. We use high-quality, pet-safe conditioning products to moisturize and nourish your dog's hair, leaving them looking and feeling their best.

Hi, I'm Liz

I started grooming at the beginning of 2015 at a corporate salon. It didn’t take long for me to realize that working corporate was not the path I wanted to take for my career. From there, I left to work at a small salon in central Minnesota. Over the last 5 years, that salon became bigger and bigger and less of what I was wanting for myself as a groomer. So when I moved to Minneapolis I found a space where I could spread my love of pets to the the cities. It is my goal at Sunshine Pet Spa to provide a calm environment so our four legged friends can enjoy the pampering they deserve.
